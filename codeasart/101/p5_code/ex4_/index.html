Exercise 4 codeasart

Reload for random speeds, colors, sizes, and tails and hats
Press T to turn off almost all hats
Press Y to turn on almost all hats
Press B to give almost everyone a special hat .........

For exercise 4, I took the bird functions I made in exercise 3 and turned them into a custom class. 3 of the birds are specifically defined in terms of location, speed, size, and if they have a hat/tail, but the rest of the birds are generated in an array that gives them random speeds, colors, sizes, and randomly decides if they initially appear with a tail and/or a hat. Within the class I also created custom actions that would allow the birds to move back and forth without writing it into the sketch code, and some actions that will toggle all the hats on, off, and swapping between colors for the birds in the array, and one of the specifically defined birds.
